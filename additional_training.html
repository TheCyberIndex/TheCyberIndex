Cybersecurity Training Resources

Below is a list of comprehensive free and paid cybersecurity training platforms to help enhance your skills in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and network security. These resources cover a wide range of topics, from beginner to expert levels.

  • HackTheBox - Online labs to practice penetration testing skills.
  • TryHackMe - Hands-on cyber labs covering a range of topics from OSINT to privilege escalation.
  • Udemy - Online courses covering a wide range of cybersecurity subjects, often at discounted prices.
  • Cisco Free NetAcad - Cisco's online academy with free cybersecurity and networking courses.
  • Fortinet Free Training - Free cybersecurity training provided by Fortinet.
  • Coursera - A platform with various cybersecurity courses, some free, others paid.
  • Cybrary - A platform offering free courses on cybersecurity and IT.

These platforms are excellent resources to help build your foundation in cybersecurity or advance your career with hands-on labs, challenges, and certifications.
