CHATBOTS
chatgpt.com

ChatGPT by OpenAI is one of the most advanced conversational AI models available. It’s based on the GPT architecture and is designed to understand and generate human-like text based on the input it receives. ChatGPT can assist in a wide range of tasks, from answering questions and drafting emails to creating content and offering personalized recommendations. Pricing varies based on usage, with options for free access under certain limitations and subscription models for more extensive use.

VIDEO CREATORS
synthesia.io

Synthesia is a leading AI-powered video creation platform that enables users to generate professional-quality videos using AI avatars. It’s particularly useful for creating training videos, marketing content, and educational materials. The platform allows users to input text, which is then converted into a video presentation with AI-generated presenters speaking in various languages. Synthesia’s pricing depends on the number of videos created and the level of customization required.

IMAGE GENERATORS
dalle-e.com

DALL-E is an AI model developed by OpenAI that generates images from textual descriptions. It’s capable of creating highly detailed and imaginative artwork based on the prompts provided by users. DALL-E can produce a wide variety of images, including abstract art, realistic landscapes, and creative concepts that push the boundaries of traditional image generation. This tool is ideal for artists, designers, and anyone in need of unique visual content. Access to DALL-E is available through OpenAI’s platform, with usage-based pricing.

LLMs LOCALLY
ollama.com

Ollama is a platform that facilitates the deployment of large language models (LLMs) locally on your own hardware. This approach is ideal for organizations and developers who need to process sensitive data or require full control over the AI models they are using. Setting up LLMs locally involves installing the necessary software (e.g., Python, specific machine learning libraries) and downloading the desired models. Ollama provides detailed guides and support to ensure a smooth installation process. This is a cost-effective and secure solution for those who require robust AI capabilities without relying on cloud services.
